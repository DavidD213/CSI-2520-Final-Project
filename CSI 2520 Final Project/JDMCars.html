JDM(Japanese Domestic Market) Cars

History

Vehicles associated with the term JDM, also known as the Japanese Domestic Market, have gained a reputation of their own in past couple of decades. Starting with it's culture, most JDM cars are seen as japanese exclusive sports cars of the late 80s and 90s. While any car exclusicely sold in japan is a JDM car, cars like the Nissan R32 and R33, the Toyota 86, and the Mazda RX-7 FC and FD were the main focus when it came to JDM cars. These JDM cars gained popularity mainly becuase of their tuneability. This meant that each car had the ability to be tuned to a specific type of driving and even racing style.
